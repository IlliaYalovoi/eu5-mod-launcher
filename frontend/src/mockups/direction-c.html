Imperium Universalis is an overhaul mod for Europa Universalis IV set in the antiquity, focusing on the rise of the Roman Empire and other great powers of the era.

Features a completely new map, hundreds of new nations, unique mechanics for the Roman Republic, and specialized trade goods appropriate for the classical period.
