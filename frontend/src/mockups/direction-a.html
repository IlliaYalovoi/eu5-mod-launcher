Imperium Universalis

Active in Playset

Total Conversion Antiquity

Imperium Universalis is an overhaul mod for Europa Universalis IV set in the antiquity, focusing on the rise of the Roman Empire and other great powers of the era.

Features a completely new map, hundreds of new nations, unique mechanics for the Roman Republic, and specialized trade goods appropriate for the classical period. The mod requires several DLCs to function properly, particularly those expanding on trade and internal mechanics.
